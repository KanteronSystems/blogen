Kanteron Presentation at the Congress of the Thai Royal College of Pathologists

27, 2020

Kanteron Systems’ CEO, Jorge Cortell, gave a presentation at the Congress of the Royal College of Pathologists of Thailand in Bangkok.

The congress took place at the Ambassador Hotel on February 27th, and Kanteron’s participation was coordinated by its distributor in Thailand, BJC.

Jorge Cortell spoke about Digital Pathology workflows and data integration.
