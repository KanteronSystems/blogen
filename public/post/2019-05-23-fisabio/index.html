Kanteron Participates in Medical Data and AI conference at FISABIO

23, 2019

Kanteron Systems CEO, participated today as member of a round table in the Medical Data and AI conference which was held at the Foundation for the Promotion of Health and Biomedical Research of the Valencian Region (FISABIO), in Spain.

The event, V edition of the ICT in Healthcare conference, is coordinated by the European Center for Innovating Companies (CEEI), the regional Bio Cluster (BIOVAL) and the Institute of Technology and Informatics (ITI), along with the regional government (GVA).
