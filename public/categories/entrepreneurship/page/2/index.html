Kanteron Systems increases stock capital by issuing new stock

1 January 2014 in Entrepreneurship, Kanteron, Kanteron News
Kanteron Systems increased its stock share capital today by issuing nearly 30% new stock, bringing it’s outstanding stock share capital to nearly half a million US dollars. This issued share capital has been subscribed by Jorge Cortell, founder and CEO of Kanteron Systems, who remains the sole proprietor of the company. This new issue of share capital is meant to provide further stability and strength so Kanteron Systems can undertake the ambitious expansion it has planned for 2014
