The report lists Kanteron Systems as one of the 12 leading companies in Healthcare’s Emerging Business Models.

<!--more-->

Titled “<a href="http://www.frost.com/sublib/display-report.do?id=K09C-01-00-00-00&src=PR" target="_blank">Healthcare 2020—Business Model Transformation Guidebook</a>” produced by Frost & Sullivan, the report indicates that around 80% of the business models in 2025 will be driven by platform-based healthcare analytics and intelligence solutions, and lists Kanteron Systems as one of the 12 leading companies in Healthcare’s Emerging Business Models (Support: Medical Imaging, Diagnostics, Pathology Laboratories, and Other Services), alongside companies like General Electric or 23 and Me.
